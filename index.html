Why Choose Us

Quran For Everybody

Our believe that the message of Quran is for everybody. There is no age threshold for the learning of Quran. The significance of Quran in the life of a Muslims is apparent from countless Hadiths. We have designed our courses in a way that caters to the needs of the adults and children alike. Our courses will enable anyone—from any background—to read the Holy Quran with Tajweed. Our syllabus is very simple. We use Noorani Qaida as our textbook for the Basic Tajweed Course. Noorani Qaida is a very small booklet. It is very helpful for the beginners. Our designers have modified it to meet the online standards. The new version is good-looking. It is also colored at certain places. These colors correspond to certain Tajweed rules at those places. We also provide videos to help students in their homework.. Our faculty consists of highly qualified and experienced teachers. They are certified by Wifaq-ul-Madaris. Moreover, the teachers have considerable experience of online as well as traditional Islamic educational systems. They are hardworking, determined, and dedicated. The teachers are open to criticism. They like to communicate with the students and/or their parents to find new ways to make the study easier and funnier.

Our Services

Services that Quran Academy Offers
Nazra course

Our Nazra course focuses on developing your skills in reading the Quran with accuracy and fluency. This comprehensive program emphasizes correct pronunciation, intonation, and articulation, enabling you to confidently recite the Quran.

Courses
Qirat course

Our Qirat course delves into the intricate art of Quranic recitation, exploring various recognized Qira'at (methods of recitation). This advanced program focuses on developing expertise in: Mastering different Qira'at, Understanding nuances of Tajweed and intonation.

Courses
Qaida course

Our Qaida course empowers you to master Quranic reading and recitation, enhancing your spiritual connection and confidence. Through this comprehensive program, you'll develop a strong foundation in Arabic alphabet, pronunciation, and Tajweed skills.
